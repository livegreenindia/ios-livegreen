LiveGreen Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 21, 2025

Summary: LiveGreen is committed to protecting your privacy. We collect only the data necessary to provide our wellness and sustainability tracking services.

1. Introduction

LiveGreen ("we", "our", or "us") operates the LiveGreen mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our app.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

Personal Information

  • Account Data: Name, email address, and profile information when you create an account
  • Authentication Data: Login credentials (securely encrypted)

Usage Information

  • Activity Data: Wellness activities you complete and track
  • Progress Data: Your completion statistics and goals
  • App Usage: Screen time tracking data (with your permission)

Device Information

  • Device Identifiers: Device type, operating system version
  • Phone State: Used for payment verification through Razorpay
  • Usage Statistics: App usage data for social media tracking feature (requires user permission)

Location Information

  • Precise Location: GPS coordinates for trek recording and outdoor activity tracking (requires user permission)
  • Background Location: Continuous location access during active trek recording to maintain accurate route tracking even when you briefly switch apps
  • Location History: Recorded trek paths including coordinates, elevation, distance, and timestamps

Health Information (via Health Connect)

  • Activity Data: Steps, distance walked, active calories burned
  • Body Measurements: Height, weight (for health calculations)
  • Vitals: Heart rate data
  • Sleep: Sleep duration and patterns
  • Nutrition: Total calories burned

Health data is only accessed when you explicitly connect Health Connect and grant permissions. This data is stored securely and never shared with third parties.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and maintain our wellness tracking services
  • Personalize your experience based on your wellness profile
  • Process payments for premium subscriptions (via Razorpay)
  • Send activity reminders and notifications (with your permission)
  • Track your progress toward sustainability goals
  • Improve our app and develop new features

4. Permissions We Request

  • Location (Precise & Background): To record trek paths and track outdoor activities. Background location is used only during active trek recording to maintain accurate tracking when you briefly switch apps. A notification is always shown during recording.
  • Health Connect: To read health metrics (steps, heart rate, calories, sleep, weight, height) for wellness tracking. All health data access requires explicit user permission.
  • Usage Access: To track social media and app screen time (optional, user-enabled)
  • Notifications: To send activity reminders
  • Phone State: Required by Razorpay for secure payment processing

5. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, database, and push notifications
  • Google Maps: Map display and location visualization for trek recording
  • Health Connect (Google): Health data integration for wellness tracking (steps, heart rate, calories, sleep)
  • Razorpay: Secure payment processing for premium subscriptions

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. Location and health data are stored securely and only used to provide app functionality.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure authentication via Firebase
  • Encrypted storage of sensitive information

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and data
  • Export your data
  • Opt-out of notifications
  • Revoke permissions at any time

8. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You can delete your account at any time through the app settings, which will remove all your personal data.

9. Account & Data Deletion

To delete your account and all associated data:

  • In-App: Go to Profile → Settings → Delete Account
  • Via Email: Send a request to support@livegreen.app

🗑️ Request Account Deletion

Upon deletion request, we will:

  • Delete your account within 7 days
  • Remove all personal information (name, email, profile)
  • Delete all activity and progress data
  • Remove any payment records (transaction IDs retained for legal compliance)
  • Send confirmation email once deletion is complete

10. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or via email.

12. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us:

Email: support@livegreen.app
